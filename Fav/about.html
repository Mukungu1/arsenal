About Arsenal FC

Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Islington, London, England. The club was founded in 1886 and has a rich history in English football.

Arsenal has won numerous domestic and international honors, including league titles and FA Cups. The club's home matches are played at the iconic Emirates Stadium.

The team is known for its attacking style of play and has produced legendary players throughout its history. The Arsenal supporters are passionate and form a significant part of the club's identity.

Key Information:

  • Founded: 1886
  • Ground: Emirates Stadium
  • Manager: Mikel Arteta
  • Colors: Red and White
